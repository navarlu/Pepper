FACULTY OF ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING (FEE CTU)
Section: Faculty Management

Description:
The management of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FEE) is responsible for coordinating and managing all study-related matters, from the design and organization of the admission procedure to the successful completion of studies. The faculty management also oversees the development of full-time study programmes in accordance with higher education requirements and the expectations of prospective students.
